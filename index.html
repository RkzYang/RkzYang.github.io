Yang’s Strategy Blog

Hi! I'm Ray Yang.
I am a strategy researcher (C.V.).

My research seeks to understand (1) firm agentic capabilities to impact and react to the external environment, and relatedly, (2) the interplay between environment-shaping strategies and performance-shaping environment. More specifically, I am interested in networked innovation ecosystems and the alpha drivers of firm market value (viz. above-normal rate of returns).

As a management theorist and a data hobbyist, I realize: (1) human brains are built to produce and consume theories, (2) data intelligence allows humans to theorize deeper, and (3) humans can achieve more by knowing how to delegate machines. As we envision and observe how things connect, our minds naturally turn to what makes them tick, producing knowledge in an accelerating loop of causal inference and probabilistic prediction.
